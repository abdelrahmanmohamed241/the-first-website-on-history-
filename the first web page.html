World Wide Web

The WorldWideWEb (W3) is a wide-area hypermedia information retrieval initiative aiming to give universal access to a large universe of documents.

Everything there is online about W3 is linked directly or indirectly to this document,including an excutive summary of the project, Mailing lists,Policy November's W3 news , Frequently Asked Questions.
